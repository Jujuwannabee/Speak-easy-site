📚 Review Key Vocabulary

Essential Words for Our Conversation

Habitat
The natural home of an animal
"The forest is the habitat of many birds."
Endangered
At risk of extinction
"Pandas are endangered animals."
Environment
The natural world around us
"We must protect our environment."
Recycle
To use something again
"We recycle plastic bottles."
Conservation
Protecting nature
"Conservation helps save animals."
Ecosystem
A community of living things
"The ocean is a large ecosystem."

🗣️ Easy Conversation (30 minutes)

Ready to Chat?

Join your instructor for a relaxed 30-minute conversation about animals and the environment. Don't worry about making mistakes - this is your safe space to practice!

Conversation Topics

🐾
What is your favorite animal? Why?
🏠
Do you have any pets?
🌍
Where do these animals live?
🌱
How can we protect the environment?
♻️
What do you recycle at home?

💬 Conversation Tips:

  • Speak naturally - don't worry about perfect grammar
  • Use the vocabulary words you learned
  • Ask questions back to your instructor
  • Share personal experiences and opinions

🌟 The Ultimate Learning Experience

Meet in Paris for an exciting "live" conversation adventure! Explore with 3 to 5 students either a museum, park, or other activity. Have fun while speaking English!

Choose Your Adventure:

🏛️

Natural History Museum

Explore animal exhibits and learn about conservation efforts while practicing English with your group.

🌳

Jardin des Plantes

Walk through beautiful gardens, discuss plant and animal life, and enjoy nature while speaking English.

🐒

Ménagerie Zoo

Visit the historic zoo, observe animals, and discuss their habitats and conservation in English.

🎉 What Makes This Special:

  • Small Groups: Maximum 5 students for personalized attention
  • Real Context: Learn English while exploring real places
  • Fun Learning: Enjoy Paris while practicing conversation
