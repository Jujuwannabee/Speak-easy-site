About Speak Easy

The Speak Easy Story

Speak Easy takes its name from the secret speakeasies of the 1920s — hidden spots where people gathered to talk freely and feel at ease. We bring that same relaxed energy to English learning.

In small, conversation-based sessions led by young, native English speakers, French teens aged 13–15 build real confidence, not just vocabulary. At Speak Easy, your child feels comfortable, connected, and ready to find confidence in every conversation.

Confidence in Every Conversation — That's our promise to every student who walks through our virtual doors.

What Sets Us Apart

💻

One-on-One Zoom Sessions

Experience personalized learning through individual Zoom sessions with native English speakers. Each session is carefully designed around engaging video content, ensuring that every conversation is relevant, meaningful, and tailored to your learning journey.

  • Personalized attention: Focus entirely on your progress and goals
  • Video-based learning: Conversations built around educational videos you've watched
  • Flexible scheduling: Learn at your own pace and convenience
  • Safe environment: Practice without fear in a judgment-free zone
🗼

Live Paris Experience

Take your English learning beyond the screen with our unique in-person experiences in Paris. Join small groups of 3-5 students to explore museums, parks, and cultural sites while practicing English in real-world contexts.

  • Real-world practice: Speak English in authentic, engaging situations
  • Cultural immersion: Discover Paris while building language confidence
  • Small groups: Maximum 5 students for personalized interaction
  • Memorable learning: Create lasting memories while improving your English

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe, comfortable space where French teenagers can practice English without fear or judgment. Through authentic conversations with native speakers, we help students build genuine confidence that extends far beyond the classroom.
