Speak Easy

Speak Easy takes its name from the secret speakeasies of the 1920s — hidden spots where people gathered to talk freely and feel at ease. We bring that same relaxed energy to English learning.

In small, conversation-based sessions led by young, native English speakers, French teens aged 13–15 build real confidence, not just vocabulary. At Speak Easy, your child feels comfortable, connected, and ready to find confidence in every conversation.

Why Choose Speak Easy?

🎭 The Speakeasy Experience

Just like the hidden speakeasies of the 1920s, we create a safe, comfortable space where teens can speak freely without judgment or pressure.

🗣️ Real Conversations

Small, intimate sessions with young native English speakers help French teens aged 13-15 build genuine confidence through authentic dialogue.

💪 Confidence Building

We focus on building real confidence, not just vocabulary. Your child will feel comfortable, connected, and ready for every conversation.
