Program Details

Program Description

Our comprehensive program is designed to improve oral expression through structured learning. Each lesson is divided into three distinct phases, culminating in a conversation with a bilingual instructor to ensure maximum learning effectiveness.

Learning Process

Step 1: Video Preparation

Watch an educational video covering the lesson topic before your session to build foundational knowledge.

Step 2: Vocabulary Building

Learn and practice the essential vocabulary list related to the lesson theme.

Step 3: Live Practice

Engage in a 30-minute to 1-hour lesson with a qualified bilingual instructor for hands-on practice.

Step 4: Live Paris Experience

Meet in Paris for an exciting "live" conversation adventure! Explore museums, parks, or other activities with a small group (3-5 students) while practicing English in real-world situations.
